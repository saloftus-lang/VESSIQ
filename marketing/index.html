The Problem

Your data arrives in
every format but one.

Maritime operations run on EDI, PDFs, CSV exports, and emails — each carrier, terminal, and forwarder with their own format. The result is data chaos that costs time and money.

EDI 315 / 310

Carrier Electronic Data

Each carrier transmits a different EDI dialect. Maersk, CMA CGM, and Evergreen all require separate parsers. One bad segment breaks your entire pipeline.

ISA*00* *00* *ZZ*MAERSK*...
GS*QM*MAERSK*PASHA*2024...
B4*I*0*MSCU1234567*20240315
PDF Invoices

Carrier & Forwarder Invoices

20+ line items per invoice — base rates, BAF, THC, ISPS, chassis, documentation fees. Each forwarder formats them differently. Manual review misses errors.

Ocean Freight 40HC ........... $3,100
BAF (Mar 2024) ............... $520
Documentation Fee ............ $75
← Not in contract
Terminal CSV Exports

Terminal & TOS Data

Terminal operating systems export raw CSV with inconsistent column names, date formats, and status codes. No two terminals produce the same schema.

CNTR_NO,STS,LOC,DT_IN...
MSCU1234567,DSCH,PIER38...
← vs CONTAINER_NUMBER, STATUS...
